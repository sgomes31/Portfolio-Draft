HELLO THERE!

My name is Sofia Gomes and I'm an aspiring Art Director currently living in Atlanta, GA. I'm a proud Brazilian - so yes, I like soccer.

Throughout my journey from Architecture school in Brazil to becoming an international student in the U.S., I have learned that there's no challenge I'm not willing to take on and that with passion and hard work, any dream is achievable.

Advertising and design are in my mind and heart 100% of the time - that is, when I'm not looking at pictures of my golden retriever.

What moves me is problem solving and expressing my creativity, and I hope to achieve my goals and aspirations trough them.
