OUTREACH BOX

Designed a custom pencil box and card as an outreach initiative to potential employers.

When combined, the pencils form the phrase:

"Hi. My name is Sofia and I'm a big admirer of your work. Now that I have yout atention, why don't you check me out? Maybe you can admire me too."

The idea is that the pencils would be used to check the blank box in the card that refers to my website.
