ZINE

My intention was to create a piece that reflected my personality as a graphic designer and commemorated Brazil's rich artistic history.

The zine is composed by a poem from celebrated Brazilian writer Fernando Pessoa, illustrated with hand made collages of pictures taken

by award-winning Brazilian photographer Sebastião Salgado. Printing and craftsmanship were hand made by me, using a mix of vellum and

pink cover paper (120 lb).
